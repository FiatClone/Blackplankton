Black Plankton Airdrop

The Black Plankton (BP) airdrop is designed to reward our most active community members and early adopters. A total of 10% of the total BP supply will be distributed to the top 100 holders after the token sale is completed.

1

Connect Your Wallet

Connect your compatible wallet to the Black Plankton platform to participate in the airdrop.

2

Acquire BP Tokens

Purchase BP tokens during the public sale or through liquidity pools once trading begins.

3

Hold Your Tokens

Maintain your BP token holdings to qualify for the airdrop. The top 100 holders will be eligible.

4

Claim Your Rewards

Once the token sale is 100% complete, eligible holders will be able to claim their airdrop rewards directly on the platform.

Important: The airdrop will be fully controlled by Black Plankton and distributed to the top 100 token holders after the sale is complete or exceeds 100% of the target.
